Fundamental Concepts and a Heuristic ExampleAuthorsAuthors and affiliationsChikio HayashiConference paper 17 Citations 6 Mentions 1.6k Downloads Part of the Studies in Classification, Data Analysis, and Knowledge Organization book series (STUDIES CLASS)SummaryData Science is not only a synthetic concept to unify statistics, data analysis and their related methods but also comprises its results. It includes three phases, design for data, collection of data, and analysis on data. Fundamental concepts and various methods based on it are discussed with a heuristic example.KeywordsNational Character Exploratory Approach Opinion Distribution Comparative Survey Cultural Sphere  These keywords were added by machine and not by the authors. This process is experimental and the keywords may be updated as the learning algorithm improves. The roundtable discussion “Perspectives in classification and the Future of IFCS” was held at the last Conference under the chairmanship of Professor H. -H. Bock. In this panel discussion, I used the phrase ‘Data Science’. There was a question, “What is ‘Data Science’? ” I briefly answered it. This is the starting point of the present paper.
